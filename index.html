The Amaatra Academy

The Amaatra Academy is a college pre-preparatory school where admissions are given from sixth grade onwards. The ultimate aim of Amaatra Academy is to equip students with all the requisite skills that will help them seek admission in some of the best educational institutions of the world.

Auditorium

Was established in 1978

The school has a beautiful auditorium in the ground floor with the seating capacity of 500 members. Most of the school literally & cultural activities are conducted in the auditorium.

Library

Was established in 1976

The school has a huge library with many books, to improve knowledge and vocabulary.Children can took their favourite books home to read.
